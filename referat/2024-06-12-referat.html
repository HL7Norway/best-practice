Mikael Rinnetmäki (Ekstern) Regarding financial resources, in my opinion all Nordic HL7 affiliates could adopt the financial model that HL7 Finland uses, with both membership fees and project fees. HL7 Finland’s project fee scales with the size of the organization (specified by amount of people involved in healthcare IT integrations). That gives the affiliate some resources that can be used to fund a FHIR ambassador and to dedicate resources to profiling work, etc.
